What is French Waffles

Don't have time to make a fancy breakfast? French Toast Waffles is a quick and easy way to make french toast out of everyone's favorite breakfast...waffles!

How to make French Waffles

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Waffles(any brand)
  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Fork
  • Frying pan

Instructions:

  1. Pour eggs and milk into bowl and stir
  2. Put waffle into bowl with mixture and flip on both sides
  3. Put frying pan on 7
  4. Place waffle into pan and flip until it's a nice golden color
  5. Let it cool and enjoy!

French Toast Waffles Recipe

Don't have time to make a fancy breakfast? French Toast Waffles is a quick and easy way to make french toast out of everyone's favorite breakfast...waffles!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Waffles(any brand)
  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Fork
  • Frying pan

Instructions

  1. Pour eggs and milk into bowl and stir
  2. Put waffle into bowl with mixture and flip on both sides
  3. Put frying pan on 7
  4. Place waffle into pan and flip until it's a nice golden color
  5. Let it cool and enjoy!

A reminder that this is only for a serving for one if you're making it for multiple people you may have to add more egg and milk

Customize it!

Use it with other breakfast foods. Try frying a hot dog or sausage and use it as a side. It's quick, safe, and enjoyable for everyone, even kids can make it!

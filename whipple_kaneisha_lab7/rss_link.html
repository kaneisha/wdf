Movie Reviews

Django Unchained

This is about a slave who was freed but a government official and goes on a search for his wife who was beaten and taken to a well known slave land.

Silver Linings Playbook

This is about two people who are recovering addicts. They fall in love even though it was unlikely and they go through obstacles along the way.

Beautiful Creatures

This is about a teenage girl who is destined to become evil because of her family lineage but she falls in love with a handsome young man and her family does everything they can to tear them apart so she can become the evil nasty creature she was destined to be.

Snitch

This is about a man who's son is put in jail for a crime he didn't commit. The father goes on a search to help uncover the truth in this drama and action filled movie. Dwayne Johnson is starring in this 4 star movie.
